Why We Exist

Quality art.
Accessible price.

Exit The Print Shop was created with one belief at its core: great art shouldn't be locked behind gallery walls and four-figure price tags. It belongs in real homes, in everyday spaces, on walls that actually mean something to the people who live with them.

We started by asking a simple question — why does owning a piece of art you love have to cost so much? The answer, usually, is that it doesn't have to. Digital files, print technology, and the ability to share work directly with people who want it have changed the game completely.

So we built ETPS. A place where you can download a high-resolution art file for the price of a coffee, take it to your local print shop, and have something genuinely beautiful on your wall by the end of the day.

Our collections are built to evolve — shaped by community feedback, current cultural trends, and a deep appreciation for art history in all its forms. From movements that redefined how we see the world, to the aesthetics defining what's happening right now. We're here for all of it.
